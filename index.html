The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 is a transformative initiative aimed at propelling the nation into a new era of innovation, sustainability, and inclusive growth. By integrating advanced technology, fostering entrepreneurship, and investing in sustainable infrastructure, Bangladesh is poised to become a leader in global development.

Digital Transformation

Building a smart and connected Bangladesh by enhancing digital infrastructure, expanding internet access, and streamlining public services through technology.

Green Energy

Driving a sustainable future with large-scale investments in solar, wind, and other renewable energy sources to reduce carbon emissions and energy dependency.

Modern Infrastructure

Developing world-class infrastructure, including smart cities, advanced transportation systems, and resilient public utilities to support long-term economic growth.

"Bangladesh stands on the threshold of remarkable transformation. Our commitment to innovation, inclusive growth, and sustainability is stronger than ever. Through collective effort, we aim to create a prosperous and resilient nation where every citizen can thrive. By embracing technology, fostering green energy, and building modern infrastructure, we are crafting a brighter future for generations to come. Together, we will elevate Bangladesh to new heights on the global stage."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
